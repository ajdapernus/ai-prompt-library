# EN

## Title
Draft email

## Prompt template
Help me write a professional email.

Recipient: [WHO, AND THEIR ROLE / RELATIONSHIP TO ME]
Goal of the email: [WHAT I WANT TO ACHIEVE]
Key points to include: [BULLET THE FACTS, REQUESTS OR DECISIONS]
Tone: [E.G. FORMAL / NEUTRAL / FRIENDLY-PROFESSIONAL]
Language: [ENGLISH / SLOVENIAN]

Rules:
- Keep it clear and concise; use short paragraphs.
- Make any request or deadline explicit.
- Suggest a subject line.
- Do not invent facts, names or figures I did not provide.

## Use case
Draft a clear, appropriately toned email — invitations, requests, follow-ups,
replies — from a few bullet points.

## Required inputs
- Recipient and your relationship to them.
- The goal of the email.
- Key points, facts or requests to include.
- Desired tone and language.

## Expected output
A ready-to-edit email with a suggested subject line, in the chosen tone and
language, covering all key points.

## Example
Recipient: external supervisor at another NCB. Goal: reschedule a working-group
call. Points: original date no longer works, propose two alternatives, keep it
friendly. → A short polite email with subject line and two proposed slots.

# SL

## Naslov
Sestavi e-pošto

## Predloga poziva
Pomagaj mi napisati profesionalno e-poštno sporočilo.

Prejemnik: [KDO IN NJEGOVA VLOGA / ODNOS DO MENE]
Cilj sporočila: [KAJ ŽELIM DOSEČI]
Ključne točke: [NAŠTEJ DEJSTVA, PROŠNJE ALI ODLOČITVE]
Ton: [NPR. FORMALEN / NEVTRALEN / PRIJAZNO-PROFESIONALEN]
Jezik: [ANGLEŠČINA / SLOVENŠČINA]

Pravila:
- Naj bo jasno in jedrnato; uporabljaj kratke odstavke.
- Vsako prošnjo ali rok navedi izrecno.
- Predlagaj zadevo sporočila.
- Ne izmišljaj si dejstev, imen ali številk, ki jih nisem navedel.

## Namen uporabe
Sestavi jasno e-poštno sporočilo z ustreznim tonom — vabila, prošnje, opomniki,
odgovori — iz nekaj alinej.

## Zahtevani vhodni podatki
- Prejemnik in vaš odnos do njega.
- Cilj sporočila.
- Ključne točke, dejstva ali prošnje.
- Želeni ton in jezik.

## Pričakovani rezultat
Sporočilo, pripravljeno za urejanje, s predlagano zadevo, v izbranem tonu in
jeziku, ki pokriva vse ključne točke.

## Primer
Prejemnik: nadzornik iz druge nacionalne centralne banke. Cilj: prestaviti klic
delovne skupine. Točke: prvotni datum ne ustreza več, predlagaj dve možnosti,
ohrani prijazen ton. → Kratko vljudno sporočilo z zadevo in dvema predlogoma.
